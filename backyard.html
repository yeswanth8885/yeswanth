ABOUT BACKYARD

A backyard is a space behind a house that is often fenced and covered with grass or other plants. Some people have decks or patios in their backyards.

Here are some ways to beautify your backyard:

  • Light it up

  • Add formal edging

  • Plant container gardens

  • Find a focal point

  • Paint your fence

  • Tidy up your lawn

  • Power-wash your pavers, deck, or patio

  • Choose the right perennials
